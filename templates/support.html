Master Schedule Help

Master Schedule is a free, open source service for creating volunteer schedules for gatherings and conferences. It was created to serve the needs of awesome groups like NASCO, who run big conferences on a shoestring budget.

We'd love to hear how your using it and whether it's meeting your needs, however, we can't guarantee that we'll be able to provide personal support. You can Contact Us and we'll do what we can.

The code base is on GitHub where you can add add issues and suggest improvements.
